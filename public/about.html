About Crafthouse Kitchen

Who We Are

The Premier Kitchen Systems Destination in Lagos

Crafthouse Kitchen is the premium kitchen brand of Sixth Column Limited, headquartered at Plot 4, Honda Close, Off Kolawole Shonibare Street, Ajao Estate, Lagos — strategically positioned near the International Airport Road.

We are dedicated to bringing world-class kitchen systems and appliances to Nigerian homes, hospitality businesses, and commercial spaces. Our extensive range spans cooker hoods, range cookers, hobs, built-in ovens, microwaves, dishwashers, refrigeration, sinks, taps, and complete kitchen storage solutions.

Every product in our catalogue is selected with one philosophy in mind: Design. Quality. Function. We partner with leading manufacturers to ensure our customers receive products that are both beautiful and built to last.
